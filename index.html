Hi, I'm Liane

I help families turn genealogy research into legacy books. Whether it's a multi-generation family history, a single ancestor's biography, or a memorial tribute, I take the records, photos, and stories you've collected and transform them into something beautiful and readable.

I founded The Lineage Studio because I believe every family's story deserves to be told with care, clarity, and craft. Too many boxes of photos and years of Ancestry research sit untouched because the jump from data to narrative feels overwhelming. That's where I come in.

I'm not just a writer or a genealogist. I'm both. And I use that combination to create books that are as meaningful as they are well-designed.
