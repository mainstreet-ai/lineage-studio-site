Frequently Asked Questions

What if I don't have much research done yet?

No problem! The Complete Biography package includes deep genealogy research. Just tell us what you know, and we'll find census records, military documents, immigration papers, and other sources to build their story.

Can you write about ancestors from the 1700s or 1800s?

Absolutely! We're experienced with historical research. The further back you go, the more we rely on historical context about the era, location, and typical life experiences alongside specific records we find.

What if there are gaps in the records?

We're honest about gaps and frame them thoughtfully. "Census records place the family in Iowa by 1880, but where they lived before remains a mystery" is narratively meaningful rather than a weakness.

Do you only write about American ancestors?

No! We research and write about ancestors from any country. Immigration stories are especially powerful.

Can I add this ancestor to my larger family history book later?

Yes! Many clients start with one ancestor biography, then expand to a multi-generation family history book. We can integrate content from earlier projects.

What file formats do you deliver?

You receive a printed hardcover or softcover book plus a high-resolution PDF you can share digitally or order additional copies through print-on-demand services.
